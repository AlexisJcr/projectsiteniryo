The robot which is presented above is the Niryo Ned² Robot, produced by the company Niryo. This robot is equiped with a six-axis robot arm and is dedicated to education, research and industry 4.0.

Ned² is designed to reproduce use cases in the industrial environment.
It allows students to take control and study a robot that could be in a production line
With its sensors and its camera, it will be able to grab objects and move them to prepared places.

Delivered with its conveyor and its work area, the robot is the optimal solution to discover, learn and prototyping Industry 4.0 processes.
